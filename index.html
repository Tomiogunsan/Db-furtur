About Us

We are a multi-disciplined team utilise over 15 years of experience, a meticulous attention to detail and a combined passion for making our clients dreams come true. All projects, including the big and the small, are handled with the same level of calibre and customer support.

We pride ourselves on creating harmonious experiences for all of our clients from the initial contact and throughout the design process. During your first meeting we’ll find out how you intend to use your spaces, your lifestyle including all of your likes and dislikes from colours to interior style. You will walk through the showroom together to further establish requirements, with the opportunity to discover many unexplored preferences. You’ll also be able to request a floor plan. Within 48 hours of receiving your brief, the Design Consultant will send you a fee proposal in line with your agreed budget.
